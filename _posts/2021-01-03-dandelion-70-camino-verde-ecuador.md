Score
-----
- Sweetness: 3
- Thickness: 2.5
- Overall: 3

Prep
----

- 1 cup (8-fl oz / 236 mL) milk
- 35g (approximately 2.5 table spoons)
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- stirred and warmed to 68–70°C (154.4-158°F)
